ASKING YOU TO TAKE RISKS WITH THINGS YOU VALUE
Free Fall High Score is the first of a line of antagonistic mobile apps. It encourages you to drop your device from high places in order to achieve the longest duration of free fall. The app records the video while falling which is submitted along with your score. But be careful, your phone must live to tell the tale in order to qualify.

Ingenious ways of saving the phone and the highest scores will be featured here.
